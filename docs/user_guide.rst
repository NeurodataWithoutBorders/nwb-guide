User Guide
===============
In the GUIDE, the process of running a conversion pipeline is broken into four high-level sections.

1. Specify the high-level structure of your conversion pipeline, including data formats and global metadata.
2. Provide your source data files and NWB File metadata on a per-subject basis to populate your files.
3. Preview your conversion before uploading to DANDI.
4. Upload your conversion to DANDI and reviewing the resulting Dandiset.

Throughout the forms found in the GUIDE, red asterisks (*) represent required properties. Attempting to move forward will throw an Error until these properties are filled in. Gray asterisks (*) are sometimes used to represent loose requirements, where missing this property will throw an Error — though you don't need to specify a value at the current stage.

Red boxes are Error messages. These will block your conversion progress until resolved.

Yellow boxes are Warning messages. Fixing them will align your NWB files with best practices.
